Bio

Bo-Hsiang Tseng (曾柏翔) is currently a PhD student in the University of Cambridge, working on Natural Language Generation (NLG), Dialogue State Tracking (DST) and End-to-end dialogue system. His supervisor is Professor Bill Byrne.

He earned a Master degree, under the supervision of Professor Lin-shan Lee in Speech Processing Lab, in the Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering from National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2016. He also holds B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from National Chiao Tung University (NCTU). His research interests include deep learning, dialogue systems, speech recognition (esp. language modeling), spoken language understanding (SLU), natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning.
